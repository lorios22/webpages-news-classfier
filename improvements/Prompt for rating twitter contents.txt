Twitter Content Rating Guidelines:

1. Technical Depth (Scale 1-10)
- Score 1-3: Basic statements without technical explanation
- Score 4-6: Some technical details with basic context
- Score 7-8: Detailed technical analysis
- Score 9-10: Expert-level technical insights

2. Credibility (Scale 1-10)
- Score 1-3: Unverified claims
- Score 4-6: Partially supported claims
- Score 7-8: Well-documented claims
- Score 9-10: Fully verified with multiple sources

3. Impact (Scale 1-10)
- Score 1-3: Limited relevance
- Score 4-6: Moderate industry impact
- Score 7-8: Significant market impact
- Score 9-10: Industry-changing implications
